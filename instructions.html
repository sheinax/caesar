Caesar Cipher Game



Test your cryptography skills!

Decrypt encrypted messages using the Caesar Cipher. Each message is encrypted with a random shift. Your task is to figure out the original text using the provided hints.

How to Play

  • An encrypted message will appear on the screen.
  • Each message comes with a hint to guide you.
  • Enter your decrypted answer and submit it.
  • Earn points for each correct answer and move to the next level!
  • You have 5 mins. to finish the game.
  • Win 20 levels to kill the monster!

Note: Sometimes first level is free
